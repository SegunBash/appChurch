About CRTM

CHRIST ROYAL TREASURE MINISTRY AKA: 'Kingdom Palace' is a Christian congregation of Christ's love Ones that passes across races, colour, ethnicity and denomination; it is more than a denomination. It is home haven for believers and heaven on earth. It is a gathering of the people of the kingdom. CRTM is a church on the "GO" (Mat 28:19-20, Mark 16:15-16, Acts 1:8). We are chosen to proclaim the wonderful acts of God (1 Peter 2:9).

CRTM VISION AND MISSION

Christ Royal Treasure Ministry means "Christ's Own people", "Christ's loved ones", "Christ's much valued people", "Christ's precious children", "Christ's special children", "Bride of Christ", "Beloved of Christ".

OUR MANDATE

To proclaim by words and actions the excellences of Christ his wonderful deeds, power love, Holiness and virtue.

OUR MISSION

Rescuing, Raising, Redirecting, Refocusing, Refuelling and Refiring people for Real Purpose.

About The General Overseer

Apst(Dr) James Okoduwa

(Senior pastor, CRTM)

About the General Overseer

Pastor James Okoduwa was born 15th March, 1963 to the family of Mr and Mrs Okoduwa Egbefoh of Udomi. Awo Uromi, Edo State. He attended Awo primary school from 1973 to 1978 where he distinguished himself among other pupils with Academic excellence. He passed common Entrance Examination into Esan Grammar School from 1978-1983.His academic performance was an outstanding one. He broke a record in the school. He maintain his excellent position out of about 200 students almost throughout his five year course in the college. Some of his classmates were Rev. Charles okoinemen of Assemblies of God, Rev Francis Omolumen of Catholic Church Owobu friday(Mechanics), August Odafen, Kenneth Okojie, Anthony Obeimen etc.

He later gave his life to Christ, when he was a mathematics teacher at Esan Grammar School, Who was a member of the Apostolic Church Awo-Uromi,under Pastor P.A momodu in the year 1984. The word and work of God was like a fire in His Heart and bone. He had a call into the ministry in 1984 immediately after his conversation. He engaged in part time pastoral, evangelistic, teaching and intercessory ministries for four (4) years 1984-1988.
On 11th November, 1988(11/11/88),he answered a call into the full time ministry in the Apostolic Church. He was posted to the Apostolic Church. Idegun Ugboha under the dynamic, loving, and caring section pastor,Micheal and Mary Erobomhan. On 3rd March 1990 (3/3/90), still at Idegun,he was wedded to the most beautiful, chaste and Holy Ghost filled girl, called "Celina Edo" at the Apostolic Church, Amendokhian, Uromi, Officiate by Pastor S.O Edogamen of blessed memory. Immediately after he graduated from Theological Seminary, he was posted to the Apostolic Church Ugun Emu, under the Superintendentship of late Apostle F.E Ogba. In 1994 he was Emu sub-section leader having four (4) churches under him: Ugun, Ibhiadan, Orakhuan, and Emunekhuan.
In 1995, He had a revelation, inspiration and urge to start a ministry was very strong, but he couldn't start, because of fear and lack of good counsel. He resigned as a pastor in the Apostolic Church and joined Christ's Own Church (COC). He worked under the superintendent of Pastor B.E Eni, a Father, a mentor and a spiritual icon from January 1994-2012. When he could not resist his call to start a ministry based on many revelations ,he resigned from Christ's Own Church on March 25th 2012, after 16 good years . (Jan 1196 - March 2012)to start Treasure of Christ Ministries.
He is an Apostolic teaching evangelist and the president and founder of Christ Mission of the World (CWM) an Interdenominational evangelist ministry that has held crusade, revival, seminar, and ministers fire conferences in so many of Edo State. He was one time a PFN, Esan North East chapter General Secretary for 4years under the chairmanship of Pastor Godfrey Iyoha. He is the current Treasurer of PFN, Esan North East chapter under the chairmanship of Rev.(Dr), F.E okhale. In July 2011, he went to Ghana for ministers conference and missionary work with some Pastors in Nigeria Rev.(Dr) Ochie (Lagos), Rev. (Dr), F. E Okhale (Uromi), Rev. Odijie (Uromi),etc..
He is a law graduate of National Open University of Nigeria.Pastor James Okoduwa is very humble and God fearing. He is an Apostle, prophet, Evangelist,Pastor and Teacher. He is a father, a motivator, a mentor and an administrator. He is a gift to these Generation.....
